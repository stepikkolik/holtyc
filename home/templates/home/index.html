URX

URX is a retro gaming console, that is meant for creativ makers.

Creativ because being creative is the main idea of our project. We want to develop a small group of URX fanatics such as we are and with them we want to more and more upgrade our console and who knows where it can gets.

Parts of URX

Main brain of URX is arduino nano every. The housing for the arduino is 3D printed. Main display is dot matrix 16x16 and secondary display is OLED 128x32. It's programmed in C.
